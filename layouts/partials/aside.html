About

My name is Joshua Born. I am an M.S.-level statistician. I would have liked to have been a professional scholar, but for many years I worked a previous career as a software developer, tried out different things, etc. etc., and life passed me by.

This blog is an outlet for me to explore my own interests in my personal time, including scholarly interests. Most of my empirical research interests are around birth control and abortion. I am also interested in critical thinking in the analysis and interpretation of empirical evidence.
